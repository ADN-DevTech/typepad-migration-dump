<p>Toshiaki Isezaki joined Autodesk in 1995 and was DevTech Japan Manager from 2000 to 2003. Toshiaki moved to the sales team as an Application Sales Engineer where he has been responsible for evangelizing our APIs and our cloud services such as Autodesk 360 and AutoCAD WS to customers. Toshiaki went back to DevTech in November 2012 and is based in the Tokyo office. </p>
<p>Before joining Autodesk, he spent his time as an engineer in CAM system company, then became a 3rd party developer for AutoCAD.</p>
