<p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/aec/joe-ye.html">Joe Ye</a></p>  <p>&#160;</p>  <p>In a view’s Visibility/Graphic Overrides dialog, click the&#160; tab &quot;Model Categories&quot;, the sub-options of &quot;Window&quot;, like &quot;glass&quot; / &quot;Frame Mullion&quot;, can controlled the visibility of the related geometries of the Window. Then how to check this in API?&#160; The Revit version is 2013.</p>  <p>We can simply call the View.SetVisibility(Category) to set the visibility of all main categories and also the sub-categories. You can simply get the target sub-category’s built-in category, and hide it. </p>  <p>Here is the code fragment to show the usage of the SetVisibility method. It hide the Glass and Frame/Mullion categories.</p>  <div style="font-family: 新宋体; background: white; color: black; font-size: 9pt">   <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; Transaction trans = <span style="color: blue">new</span> Transaction(doc);</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; trans.Start(<span style="color: #a31515">&quot;ChangeVisibility&quot;</span>);</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; <span style="color: green">//Hide glass sub-category</span></p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; doc.ActiveView.setVisibility(doc.Settings.Categories.get_Item(</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; BuiltInCategory.OST_WindowsGlassProjection),<span style="color: blue">false</span>);</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; <span style="color: green">//Hide the frame/mullion sub-categories</span></p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; doc.ActiveView.setVisibility(doc.Settings.Categories.get_Item(</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; BuiltInCategory.OST_WindowsFrameMullionProjection),<span style="color: blue">false</span>);</p>    <p style="margin: 0px">&#160;&#160;&#160; trans.Commit();&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </p> </div>  <p>To get the visibility status of sub-categories, simply call View.GetVisibility(Category) method.</p>
