<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/aec/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>
<p>This is an exciting opportunity to be part of Forge &amp; ADN team of Autodesk in <strong>Shanghai or Beijing, China</strong>. We are a global team, providing consulting services to worldwide developers &amp; partners of Autodesk. We are running two programs: <a href="http://autodesk.com/adn">Autodesk Developer Network (ADN) </a>and <a href="http://forge.autodesk.com/">Autodesk Forge</a>. The former is mainly for APIs of traditional desktop products, the second is for the brand new web services.</p>
<p>We &#0160;evangelize Autodesk API &amp; web services, help Autodesk partners to find the right technologies, assist them to make things differently, more successful and be consistent developing in the eco-system of Autodesk. This will positively impact Autodesk’s business. You will provide technical assistance including application designing, customizing, trouble shooting, writing samples, training and mentoring etc.</p>
<p>Autodesk has a breadth software across designing, visualizing, collaborating, making and using etc. We have transited to the cloud and is providing APIs through those cloud-based services. We are looking for someone to provide evangelistic API support for <strong>Revit</strong>, and we also hope to find someone excited by the world in the cloud.</p>
<p>Those who work or study abroad and want to pursue their career in China are also welcome! (mandarin is required)</p>
<p><strong>Requirements</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelors&#39; degree or higher in computer science, architecture, structure, civil engineering, BIM or related field.</li>
<li>At least 2 years hands-on programming experience (including time in university for a concrete research project).</li>
<li>At least 1 year hands-on experience on <strong>Revit API</strong>. Skillful in .NET programming.</li>
<li>Intelligent mind and ability to quickly learn new software development technologies. Enthusiastic in experimenting and writing sample code.</li>
<li>Written and verbal English skills are important. Ability to be patient and communicate across cultures. Other language fluency is also a great asset.</li>
<li>Good team player: Easy to work with team members across various cultures.</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot software and programming problems. Good understanding of techniques to debug software will be of great value.</li>
<li>Enjoy interacting with people – including one-on-one conversations and presenting to audiences at conferences.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge on various technologies, especially web/cloud/mobile developing is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with other Autodesk products and their APIs will be valuable.</li>
<li>Experience of BIM, Operation Maintenance and IoT will be plus.</li>
</ul>
