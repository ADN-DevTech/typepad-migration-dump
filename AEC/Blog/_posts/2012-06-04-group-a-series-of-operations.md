<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/aec/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p><b>Issue</b></p>  <p>I will call a series of functions/methods. Some of them will take effect at once on the model. So the speed becomes slow. I want to group these operations and just update the model once.</p>  <p><b>Solution</b></p>  <p>With COM, you can use state.BeginEdit, do all the calls, and then call state.EndEdit. Calling BeginEdit/EndEdit bundles up undo information. It also bundles up internal notifications.
