<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/aec/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p>This is a little late news because <a href="http://apps.exchange.autodesk.com/NAVIS/en/Home/Index">Navisworks Exchange Store</a> has opened for some weeks :-) We have seen a bunch of downloading with the published Apps so far. I believe some developers have already known about it and are eager to submit their applications soon. </p>  <p>If you are not familiar with Autodesk Exchange Apps you might want to visit <a href="http://apps.exchange.autodesk.com/en">Exchange Store</a> to get an idea of all the current offerings and possibilities for you. The stores serve all customer types whether they need plug-ins, standalone applications, content libraries, utilities, training material or other products that can help them in their daily work.</p>  <p>Exchange Apps has proven to be the most effective way for app publishers to get exposure to Autodesk customers.&#160; It is a safe and easy way for customers to find the tools they need for their work. Exchange Apps is growing – there are more than 1,000 apps and in 2013 there were over a million unique visitors to Exchange Apps, and 334,000 unique downloads. It is a global opportunity and community with stores in 14 languages. Autodesk is implementing several features to support the sale of apps – volume sales, term/subscription based sales, web services, and purchase orders.</p>  <p>The <a href="http://usa.autodesk.com/adsk/servlet/index?siteID=123112&amp;id=17183245">Knowledge Base</a> includes the requirements of each product and the detail tutorial on submission process.</p>  <p>Navisworks Manage/ Simulate 2015 and onward support Apps delivered by Autodesk Exchange. Apps can be used to add both.NET API plugins and NWcreate plugins. <a href="http://usa.autodesk.com/adsk/servlet/item?siteID=123112&amp;id=23096389">Information for Navisworks app developers</a> can help you to get started with.</p>  <p>We are looking forward to seeing your App on the shelf !</p>  <p>&#160;</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01a3fd03d654970b-pi"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="image" border="0" alt="image" src="/assets/image_486012.jpg" width="289" height="238" /></a></p>
