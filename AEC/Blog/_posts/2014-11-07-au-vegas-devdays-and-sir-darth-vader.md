<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/aec/jaime-rosales.html">Jaime Rosales</a></p>
<p>We are only 22 days away from one of the greatest and biggest events Autodesk has to offer in the US, Autodesk University, and Autodesk API developers cannot miss such opportunity to interact with our expert evangelists during <a href="http://autodeskdevdays.com/las-vegas/" target="_blank">DevDays in Las Vegas, Nevada</a>. If you are one of the many enthusiastic developers using Autodesk API’s you don’t want to miss this chance.</p>
<p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01bb07a7a7e1970d-pi"><img alt="DevDays2b" border="0" height="294" src="/assets/image_676754.jpg" style="display: inline; border-width: 0px;" title="DevDays2b" width="480" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://autodeskdevdays.com/" target="_blank">DevDays</a> tour started in the beginning of November and will go through January in 16 cities around the globe. ADN Leader Jim Quanci and expert evangelist Philippe Leefsma will be attending all 16 cities to bring you a lot of excitement about what’s new for developers using Autodesk Technologies. Get a <a href="http://autodeskdevdays.com/devdays-2014/" target="_blank">sneak peak here</a>.</p>
<p>I will be attending AU Vegas from Dec 1st until Dec 4th, please feel free to stop by and say Hi when ever you see me, trust me is not that hard to miss me.</p>
