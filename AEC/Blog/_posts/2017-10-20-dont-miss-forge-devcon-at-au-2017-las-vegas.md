<p>Have you registered for Forge DevCon at AU 2017 Las Vegas?&#0160;&#0160;If you haven’t and planning to come, here are a few pointers to register. (It may not be obvious how to add DevCon afterward.):</p>
<ol>
<li>Register to Forge DevCon through the&#0160;<a href="http://au.autodesk.com/las-vegas/registration-pricing?utm_source=Content&amp;utm_medium=Blog&amp;utm_campaign=DevConLV">AU registration page</a>.
<ul>
<li>Combined ticket includes Forge DevCon – $2,175</li>
<li>Forge DevCon standalone ticket – $295<br />(AU is very expensive, isn’t it?)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>If you already have a ticket to AU and want to add Forge DevCon.
<ul>
<li>You can add to the AU full price ticket – $95</li>
<li>Call customer service at call 888-371-1722 (toll-free in the United States) or 415-446-7717.</li>
<li>Or send an email to customer service&#0160;<a href="mailto:autodeskuniversity@autodeskevents.com">autodeskuniversity@autodeskevents.com</a>.<br />They will add for you.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p>For more detail about what Forge DevCon is about, please take a look at <a href="https://forge.autodesk.com/blog/developing-autodesk-desktop-software-heres-why-you-should-care-about-forge-devcon-and-autodesk">this post</a>.&#0160;</p>
