<p>If you are writing Revit add-ins for more than a year, most likely you are already familiar with our annual API wishlist survey. I have been working with the Revit product manager to assemble this year&#39;s API wishlist survey. Here is it the link to the this year&#39;s survey:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/RevitAPI2014Spring" target="_blank" title="Revit API wishlist survey spring 2014">Revit API Wishlist Survey - Spring 2014 </a></p>
<p>Please take a moment to participate to this survey. It will take less than 5 minutes.&#0160;The Revit product team will use the result of this survey to prioritize the areas to focus for API enhancement. We would also encourage you to give us a feedback you may have using a free form feedback section.&#0160;If you provide the e-mail address when filling the survey, we will send you the result after the survey. &#0160;(We will not sure your e-mail address with anybody else other than the purpose of this survey.) &#0160;</p>
<p>The survey will be open for three weeks till<strong> June 20th, 2014</strong>. We look forward to hearing from you!</p>
