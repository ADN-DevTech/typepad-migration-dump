<p>Thank you to the 179 of you who took the time to complete this year&#39;s AutoCAD API Wishlist survey. We changed things around&#0160;this year&#0160;by asking&#0160;some more forward looking questions, instead of the usual &#39;vanilla&#39;&#0160;feature hitlist. I&#39;ll be analyzing the survey data and passing the information on to our product management and development teams to help them make decisions about future features and APIs.</p>
