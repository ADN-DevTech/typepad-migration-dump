I understood acdbSetReferenced() should do this, but my blocks are still being purged after calling it. What should I do?</p>  <p><a name="section2"></a></p>  <p><b>A:</b></p>  <p>Purging of anonymous blocks is done immediately after the drawing containing the block has been loaded.
