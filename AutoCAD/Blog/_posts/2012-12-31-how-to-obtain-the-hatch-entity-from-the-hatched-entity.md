<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/gopinath-taget.html">Gopinath Taget</a></p>  <p>You might wonder, when an entity is hatched, is there is an associative entity created that is the hatch (an AcDbHatch)? And how do I locate this object?</p>  <p>The way the hatch responds to changes in the entity is through notification. The hatch is actually made a persistent reactor of the entity in the hatch. You can then obtain the hatch entity in this way.
