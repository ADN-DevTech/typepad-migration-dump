<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/stephen-preston.html">Stephen Preston</a></p>  <p><em>Slightly late to the party with this one (Kean blogged about this a few days ago), but anyway …</em></p>  <p>Here’s a chance to win <strong>US$5000</strong> (and a lot of publicity) for your next great idea for an AutoCAD app.</p>  <p>APPHACK @ AU is a virtual code-fest to produce apps/plugins for AutoCAD using the ObjectARX and/or .NET APIs, and it's open to any ADN member who can produce a working app/plugin for AutoCAD. The event culminates in a live demo at Autodesk University in Las Vegas on November 26, 2012.&#160; Simply submit your app and a demo video and you're in the running for some nice prizes. But hurry - the deadline for submissions is 11:59pm Pacific on Wednesday, 14 November, 2012.</p>  <p>First prize is US$5000, Second prize is US$2000, and Third prize is US$1000. Ten finalists will be awarded a newly released tablet computer valued at approximately $500.</p>  <p>Submit something you've already written or write something new. The theme for the event is <b>Connecting AutoCAD to the Cloud</b>.</p>  <p>In order to win, you must be present to perform a live demo of your app in Las Vegas on November 26. Click on the link below to visit the registration site for full details and the rules of engagement.</p>  <p><a href="http://promoshq.wildfireapp.com/website/6/contests/296215"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="clip_image001" border="0" alt="clip_image001" src="/assets/image_781677.jpg" width="482" height="99" /></a></p>
