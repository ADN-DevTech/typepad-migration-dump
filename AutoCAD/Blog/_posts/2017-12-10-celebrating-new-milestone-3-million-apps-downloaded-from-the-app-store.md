<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/Madhukar-Moogala.html" target="_self">Madhukar Moogala</a></p>
<p>We are proud and excited to announce that Autodesk App Store has exceeded <strong>Three </strong>million downloads.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It is a huge milestone and we extend a big thank you to all our publishers for your contribution to the success of Autodesk App Store.</p>
<p>We encourage you to share the news with your customers too.</p>
<p>In addition to rapid growth in downloads, activity is growing across the board.&nbsp;</p>
<p>There are now over 2950 apps in the store with 1200+ AutoCAD and its vertical apps, 900+ Revit Apps, 500+ Manufacturing apps and 250+ Media and Entertainment apps.</p>
