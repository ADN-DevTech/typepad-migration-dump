<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/stephen-preston.html">Stephen Preston</a></p>
<p>Last November, I encouraged you all (yawl?) to <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/2012/11/vote-for-visual-studio-x64-edit-and-continue.html" target="_blank">vote on the Microsoft Uservoice site for the &#39;x64 edit and continue&#39; request</a>.</p>
<p>Good News! Today, I (and all my DevTech colleagues) received an email confirming that this will become available (relatively soon) in Visual Studio 2013:</p>
<p>

<p>Well - mostly good news - this is for VB.NET and C# only. <a href="http://visualstudio.uservoice.com/forums/121579-visual-studio/suggestions/4126415-x64-edit-and-continue-for-c-" target="_blank">The request for C++ is still open</a>.</p>
<p>Nevertheless, all of us who voted for this should take a moment to pour a celebratory glass of Mountain Dew Code Red, smile smugly, and congratulate ourselves for our powers of persuasion and general awesomeness. (And then go and vote for the equivalent C++ request :-).</p>
