<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/gopinath-taget.html">Gopinath Taget</a></p>  <p>The same restrictions apply to dictionary keys as to symbol table names: 255 characters or fewer, and they may be alphanumeric, or a dollar symbol ($), underscore (_) or hyphen (-). Use acdbSNValid() to check the validity of a symbol table name. </p>  <p>The length again depends on the sysvar &quot;EXTNAMES&quot;. For more details, please refer to the online documentation.</p>
