In fact this feature is not available on the API, mainly because it can potentially represent a big use of memory. But a (partial) workaround may help in some cases!</p>  <p>The main thing is that is not easy to track everything that change on a entity, mainly because one can refer to another, or even contain internal data not easily exposed.
