<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/stephen-preston.html">Stephen Preston</a></p>  <p>I think this is the last AutoCAD 2013 <a href="http://www.google.com/search?q=autoloader&amp;domains=through-the-interface.typepad.com&amp;sitesearch=through-the-interface.typepad.com" target="_blank">Autoloader</a> enhancement to document now. Its more of an incremental enhancement than the others …</p>  <p>We strongly recommend that you set your plug-ins to load on demand (usually LoadOnCommandInvocation), rather than on startup. This is so a customer who has installed lots of plug-ins doesn’t see a huge slowdown in AutoCAD startup times. That wasn’t so easy for LISP apps – until now.
