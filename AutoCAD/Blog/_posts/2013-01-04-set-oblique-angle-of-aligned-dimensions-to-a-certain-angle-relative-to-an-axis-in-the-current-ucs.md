<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/gopinath-taget.html">Gopinath Taget</a></p>  <p>Consider this: You want to set the oblique angle of an aligned dimension to an arbitrary value (for example, 30 degrees from the X-axis of the current UCS to one of the extension line within which the dimension is constructed).</p>  <p>To do this, you need to realize that that the Oblique angles are calculated relative to the line containing the two start points of the two extension lines of the dimension. Let's call this the base line of aligned dimensions.
