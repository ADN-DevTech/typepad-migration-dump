<p>Through this post I am sharing a nice sample on draw over rule and grip over rule. This sample was written in 2009/2010 by Stephen Preston for AutoCAD 2010, I have migrated the version to work with AutoCAD 2013.&nbsp; The sample basically overrules the lines draw functionality and shows the same line as thermometer.&nbsp; The sample also shows the procedure to use grip overrule.</p>
<p>To use the sample:</p>
<p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/files/sgp_overruletest_xdict.zip">Download SGP_OverruleTest_XDict</a><br />build the sample<br />Start AutoCAD 2013, NETLOAD the sample<br />Draw a line<br />Issue command “ADDDATA” and provide required inputs.</p>
