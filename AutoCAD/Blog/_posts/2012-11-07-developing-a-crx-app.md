<p><strong>Q2: How do I register my CRX app for demand loading (e.g. on command invocation)?</strong></p>  <p>A2: You register them exactly as you would for an AutoCAD app. The only difference is that AcCoreConsole will not load an ObjectARX DLL with the extension ‘ARX’. You have to rename the extension to ‘CRX’.</p>
