rEvolution! Announcing ADN DevDays 2013

The Autodesk Developer Network is proud to present DevDays 2013! As you may already know, my team is part of the Autodesk Developer Network program at Autodesk. We support all major products that have APIs from the Autodesk product portfolio. To keep professional developers up-to-date, we often provide information about new products before they hit the streets. This is what the DevDays events are all about!  Note you need to be an ADN member to attend. See below for details. I will be presenting 3ds Max API updates at all the M&E dates listed at the bottom of this post. Let me know if you are attending and I would be happy to meet you! Hope to see you there!

rEvolution

Where Desktop meets the Cloud

Got great ideas for cloud and mobile apps? Want to learn where Autodesk is taking the convergence of desktop computing and cloud technology?

If you’re an innovator developing on Autodesk platform technology and have not yet attended an Autodesk Developer Network Conference join us for a deep dive as we take an in-depth look at Autodesk’s newest technologies and the next generation of products coming in 2014. The Autodesk Developer Network is holding its annual, worldwide Developer Day Conference and DevLab in cities around the world from November through December 2013.

Called Autodesk DevDays 2013, the theme for this year’s event is rEvolution – Where Desktop meets the Cloud. Come and talk to us about future web services from Autodesk. We will of course also cover what’s new in the next releases of our desktop products (3ds Max®, Maya®, AutoCAD®, Revit®, Inventor® and so on)!

Note that material at Developer Days is forward-looking and highly confidential so ADN membership is required. Maybe you are using ADN Open and forums and would like to attend DevDays? Visit www.autodesk.com/joinadn or send an email to your local ADN representative and let’s talk. We like to hear from — and help — interested parties, innovators and companies just getting started.

Below are the dates for this year’s M&E DevDays tour that will include information for 3ds Max, Maya, and MotionBuilder.

- Nov. 7, 2013 - Beijing, China, with DevLab on Nov. 8

- Nov. 12, 2013 - Shanghai, China afternoon with DevLab in the morning.

- Nov. 14, 2013 - Tokyo, Japan

- Dec. 2, 2013 - Las Vegas, USA, with DevLab on Dec. 3

For a more detailed agenda for US AU DevDays and to register your interest in attending - see here.
Detailed agenda for M&E DevDays at US AU posted here.
For China or Japan events, contact your local ADN resource for registration details.

 [EDIT] - Please note the change to the Shanghai date above. It is now entirely on Nov. 12th.
