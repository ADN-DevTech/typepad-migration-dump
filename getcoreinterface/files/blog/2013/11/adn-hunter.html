ADN Hunter

Wow! Where I have I been for the past month?!? Sorry for not posting as quickly as I would have liked. But basically we are busy with the annual DevDay conferences. I just returned from China and Japan where we kicked off the event dates this year. That leads me to ask if you have registered for the free M&E Sparks ADN Developer Conference next Monday, Dec. 2nd/3rd in Las Vegas? If not see here: M&E DevDays are coming soon!

If you are going, pay close attention to this next part! Win Prizes! Announcing ADN Hunter!

Photograph rare ADN animals at DevDay, DevLab and AU for a chance to win an iPad Mini! These animals are hard to spot, but if you attend the conference, you will have many opportunities. Basically photo-hunting any of us ADN employees, will gain you chances to win!

We would LOVE to meet you and talk with you about the amazing things you are doing with Autodesk technology. We encourage you to say “hi” and to make it fun, we’re running a small competition. The rules are simple:

  • Download the AU Mobile App (from Google Play or Apple Store)
  • Track down as many members of the ADN team on “The Hunted” list as you can
  • In the AU Mobile App click status update 
  • Take a photo of yourself with an ADN team member and post it using the hashtag #ImWithADN

The prizes (3 iPad Minis) will be awarded based on who posts the most pictures over the duration of AU. Extra points will be given (at the judges’ discretion) for imaginative photo composition, also if there is a tie, creativity will be rewarded!

Set up instructions:

  1. Download the AU Mobile App from the App Store or Google Play
    Note: No Blackberry® or Windows Store app is available, but you can use the HTML5 (web browser) version on these devices or your laptop. All apps can be accessed from here: http://doubledutch.me/download/autodesk-university-2013
  2. Find the AU Mobile App "Settings", then select Connect to Twitter

The Hunted List:

Jim Quanci, Stephen Preston, Cyrille Fauvel, Silvio Guido, Peter Schlipf, Natalia Polikarpova, Hazel Wood, Denis Cadu, Mikako Harada, Kevin Vandecar, Gopinath Taget, Augusto Goncalves, Brian Ekins, Xianhua Tang, Adam Nagy, Philippe Leefsma, Jeremy Tammik, Arvind Thangli

Note that I am willing to help you with the "creative" aspect as long as it does not involve removing my clothes or anything else that would typically "stay in vegas". ;-)

Good luck, and have a great time at the conference!
