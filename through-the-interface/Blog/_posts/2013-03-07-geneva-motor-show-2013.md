<p>As I had to meet someone at Geneva airport, this morning, I decided to kill two birds with one stone and spend a few hours visiting <a href="http://www.salon-auto.ch/en" target="_blank">the 83rd International Motor Show</a>.</p>
<p>The show opened its doors to the public, today, and includes such stunning vehicles as <a href="http://editorial.autos.msn.com/lamborghini-veneno-stakes-supercar-claim-in-geneva" target="_blank">the $4.6 million Lamborghini Veneno</a>:</p>
<p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2017ee905c49d970d-pi" target="_blank"><img alt="Lamborghini Veneno" border="0" height="320" src="/assets/image_70442.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 20px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Lamborghini Veneno" width="470" /></a></p>
<p>Yes, that wasn’t a typo. This car would set you back the best part of $5 million. Ouch.</p>
<p>I went quickly past most of the stands, though, and spent most of my time at Morgan Motor Company’s. As <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2012/12/midlife-crisis-planning.html" target="_blank">I’ve mentioned before</a>, MMC is a big user of Autodesk software. I also happen to like their cars a great deal.</p>
