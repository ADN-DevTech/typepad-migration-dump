<p>There’s still time to participate in the <a href="http://www.exchangeapphack.com" target="_blank">Autodesk Exchange Apps Hackathon</a>, a virtual event taking place this weekend (September 20-21). The point of <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2014/08/autodesk-exchange-apps-hackathon-2014.html" target="_blank">this event</a> is to encourage developers to post apps to the <a href="https://apps.exchange.autodesk.com/en" target="_blank">Autodesk Exchange Apps</a> store, and we’re even paying cool, hard cash ($50 or $100, whether free or paid) for each app that gets published.</p>  <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201a73e17cf70970d-pi" target="_blank"><img title="Hackathon" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 20px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" border="0" alt="Hackathon" src="/assets/image_292744.jpg" width="474" height="291" /></a></p>  <p>Presentations and discussions will include:</p>  <ul>   <li>How to architect your app for Exchange:</li>    <ul>     <li>How to build your AutoCAD® app</li>      <li>How to build your Autodesk® Revit® app</li>      <li>How to build your Autodesk® Inventor® app</li>      <li>How to build your Autodesk® 3ds Max® and Autodesk® Maya® apps</li>   </ul>    <li>Handling IPN notifications</li>    <li>Implementing simple copy protection for your app</li>    <li>Architecting your app to sell on monthly subscription</li>    <li>Selling online web services on Exchange Apps</li>    <li>Making use of Autodesk ‘cloud’ services such as our new, web-based, zero-client 3D model viewer</li>    <li>Sustainability apps</li> </ul>  <p>Lots of great information to help kick-start your app development. <a href="http://www.eventbrite.com/e/autodesk-exchange-apps-hackathon-registration-12477222709?ref=ebtnebregn" target="_blank">Sign up now!</a></p>
