<p>My friend and colleague on the ADN team, Lisa Senauke, helped get a bunch of special-branded VR viewers for the upcoming <a href="http://forge.autodesk.com/conference" target="_blank">Forge DevCon</a>. If you’re coming along and don’t get one from attending my class, be sure to swing by the exhibit hall and pick one up. This is the latest “Google Cardboard”-style device from our friends at DODOcase and should even fit 6” smartphones (a problem with the previous generation).</p> <p>They look <u>really</u> cool, even if I do say so myself (I spent a few wild hours inside PhotoShop to create the layout, believe it or not).
