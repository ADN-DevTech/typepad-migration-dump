<p>On Wednesday morning I headed off, bright and early, to the <a href="https://www.keanw.com/2019/08/the-dbei-hackathon-in-edinburgh-can-you-hack-it.html" target="_blank">Generative Design Hackathon</a> that had been organised by <a href="https://www.dbeinstitute.org/" target="_blank">DBEI</a> prior to this year&#39;s <a href="https://www.dbeinstitute.org/event/bilt-europe-2019/" target="_blank">BILT Europe in Edinburgh</a>.</p>
