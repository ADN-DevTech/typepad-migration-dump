<p>I’m back in Barcelona for this spring’s <a href="http://autodeskcloudaccelerator.com" target="_blank">Forge Accelerator</a>. It’s been an interesting week: I arrived on Tuesday morning – having missed the first day in an attempt to shave a little time off my <a href="http://www.keanw.com/2018/05/one-mans-trash-bjarne-stroustrups-the-c-programming-language.html" target="_blank">June travel craziness</a> – and arrived to find a number of attendees working on projects related to (and inspired by) the work we’ve done in <a href="http://dasher360.com" target="_blank">Dasher 360</a>. This is really encouraging… I’m thrilled to see others using Forge to tackle the kind of problems Dasher was always intended to help address. Onwards!</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2022ad398d787200b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Attendees" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Attendees" src="/assets/image_184822.jpg" border="0"></a><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2022ad398d78f200b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="More attendees" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="More attendees" src="/assets/image_497022.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>The weather on Tuesday wasn’t great – a little windy and grey – but since then it’s been lovely. I found the nearest Bicing stand and picked up a heavy, but functional shared bike.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2022ad35301b5200c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Bicing bikes" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Bicing bikes" src="/assets/image_639648.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>I plugged “Poble Sec” into Google Maps and headed on over. All was going very well until the road started heading uphill in a serious way. It turns out that the centre of Poble Sec is up on the side of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montju%C3%AFc" target="_blank">Montjuic</a>, Barcelona’s mountain. I only had offline maps, so was following driving directions for cars: the driving directions for bikes would have gone around the mountain, logically enough. So it was that I ended up sweating my way up onto Montjuic on my heavy rent-a-bike. Ah well, you live and learn.</p><p>David and I had a nice lunch and I biked back across to the Autodesk office.
