<p>Our path to Malaysia from Cairns took us via Sydney, where we had to recheck bags and change terminal. The flight was delayed by over an hour because of a medical emergency – not on the plane itself, someone in a stretcher would be occupying a section of the plane as it flew onwards to Kuala Lumpur. It was a little strange seeing the curtained-off stretcher in the economy cabin: it’s not something I’d seen before. I hope the person arrived safely and is getting the treatment they need.</p><p>The flight itself was about 8.5 hours. Over half of the flight-time was spent crossing Australia – this country is huge! At one point – purely by chance – I opened the sunshade just as we were about to leave the mainland and head out across the ocean. It helped me appreciate Australia’s vastness.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2beb98a970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="417" title="Leaving Australia" style="margin: 30px auto; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Leaving Australia" src="/assets/image_17725.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b7c9345fb0970b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Sydney to KL" style="margin: 30px auto; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Sydney to KL" src="/assets/image_290918.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>Arriving in KL we were greeted by Matt, one of my oldest friends. Getting there was complicated by our UberXL driver having trouble staying connected to the network – Uber has had issues in Malaysia on this front, for some reason – but we got there eventually.</p><p>Muna knows the hikes in this area really well, and was the perfect guide for a late morning (and very sweaty) walk through the jungle.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2beb9d3970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Walking through the jungle near Puchong" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Walking through the jungle near Puchong" src="/assets/image_1559.jpg" border="0"></a><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2beb9d7970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="375" height="500" title="Some steep parts" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Some steep parts" src="/assets/image_231385.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2beb9e3970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="375" height="500" title="Beautiful" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Beautiful" src="/assets/image_700429.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b7c934600d970b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Some great ferns" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Some great ferns" src="/assets/image_859965.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>Muna took us back into KL for a lovely lunch at <a href="https://www.isabel.com.my/" target="_blank">Isabel</a>. The food in this city is just unbelievably good!</p><p>We spent a few hours digesting, back at the flat, before heading out to visit the city again. The area down by the river is lit up in the evening.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201bb09d78350970d-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Down by the river" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Down by the river" src="/assets/image_600585.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>We spent some time at an interactive art exhibit, which involved LEDs, water and selfies.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2bebb77970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="375" height="500" title="Interactive art" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Interactive art" src="/assets/image_291060.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>We walked past the opening of the <a href="https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2017/11/303006/2017-negaraku-expo-getting-final-touches-wednesday-opening" target="_blank">Expo Negaraku 2017</a>, where <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Najib_Razak" target="_blank">Najib Razak</a>, Malaysia’s controversial Prime Minister, was addressing the assembled crowd. We had two types of crab – one with black pepper and one with a curry leaf and tamarind sauce. Incredible.</p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201bb09d7835a970d-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="A last Malaysian dinner" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="A last Malaysian dinner" src="/assets/image_333505.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b8d2bebb80970c-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="375" title="Our two crabs" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Our two crabs" src="/assets/image_827004.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>One the way home Matt picked us up some durian to have for breakfast.
