<p>As I inexorably head towards the end of my 30s (just a few months to go, now), I feel more and more justified to get cracking on a proper <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midlife_crisis" target="_blank">midlife crisis</a>. And I’ve been told that to get the most out of a good crisis, you really need to invest in at least a little crisis planning. :-)</p>
<p>My most recent bout of rumination kicked off at AU2012, when – to my huge surprise – I stumbled across a new <a href="http://www.morgan3wheeler.co.uk" target="_blank">Morgan 3 Wheeler</a>. Here’s a picture of the car with Jon Wells, the Senior Designer at Morgan Motor Company Ltd., who <a href="http://www.morgan-motor.co.uk/mmc/newspages/vegas.html" target="_blank">also happened to speak at this year’s AU</a>:</p>
<p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2017d3ec06f55970c-pi" target="_blank"><img alt="Jon Wells with the Morgan 3 Wheeler" border="0" height="332" src="/assets/image_295976.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 20px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Jon Wells with the Morgan 3 Wheeler" width="477" /></a></p>
<p>Now I’m the first to admit I’m not hugely bothered about cars. I’ve had good cars and bad, over the years, and I’m not someone who obsesses about engine specifications, etc. But I’ve dreamed of owning a Morgan – and of <a href="http://www.morgan3wheeler.co.uk/history.html" target="_blank">the three-wheeled variety</a> – since I can remember, and the fact that they’re now being made again after a 60-year hiatus, no-less, is simply fantastic news.</p>
<p>The above model – and the one that I’m most tempted by – is <a href="http://www.morgan3wheeler.co.uk/superdryedition.html" target="_blank">the Superdry Edition</a>, of which <a href="http://www.morgan3wheeler.co.uk/news.php?id=26" target="_blank">they’re only making 200</a>. If you’d like to see a little more about how these beautiful machines were designed (with heavy use of <a href="http://autodesk.com/alias" target="_blank">Alias</a>, <a href="http://autodesk.com/showcase" target="_blank">Showcase</a> and <a href="http://autodesk.com/3dsmax" target="_blank">3ds Max</a> along the way) and built, you should check out this video:</p>
