<p>Well, we made it to episode 60! Just four more to go after <a href="https://youtu.be/2MhJMoyO65s" rel="noopener" target="_blank">today’s episode</a>. Today we ended up playing <a href="https://www.c64-wiki.com/wiki/Eagle_Empire" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Eagle Empire</a> – a clone of the class arcade game, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phoenix_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Phoenix</a> – and then we played an old favourite again: <a href="https://www.keanw.com/2021/08/floppy-friday-43.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">last time</a> it was called Pogo, while this time it went by its more usual name of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nebulus_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Nebulus</a>. Then we played a somewhat disappointing <a href="https://www.lemon64.com/games/details.php?ID=1913" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Petch</a> before failing to load <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Dam_Busters_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Dam Busters</a> and finishing up by actually playing the rather good <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Knight_Games" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Knight Games</a>.</p>
