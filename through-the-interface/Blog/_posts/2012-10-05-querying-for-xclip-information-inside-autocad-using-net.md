<p>This question came in recently by email:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>I have reached a snag when trying to find the boundaries of external references that have been &quot;xclipped&quot; by the user. Or, to be more precise, I can&#39;t even really find the data telling me whether or not the external reference has been &quot;xclipped&quot; at all. I&#39;m wondering if you have any idea how or where I could find this data.</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>While I found <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2010/11/adding-a-2d-spatial-filter-to-perform-a-simple-xclip-on-an-external-reference-in-autocad-using-net.html" target="_blank">this previous post</a> showing how to perform an XCLIP, I couldn’t find anything showing how to query for XCLIP information.</p>
