<p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2016760ba3b67970b-pi" target="_blank"><img style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 20px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Russian Homestyler" border="0" alt="Russian Homestyler" src="/assets/image_218468.jpg" width="474" height="171" /></a>I mentioned some time ago that our Localization Services (LS) team <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/11/autodesk-collaborative-translation-platform.html" target="_blank">was embarking on a community-based translation</a> of <a href="http://www.homestyler.com" target="_blank">Homestyler</a> into Russian.</p>  <p>The fruits of this labour are now ready for testing: the various translation projects, for the application, for its content and for supporting web-pages are now 100% complete. But that’s not to say things haven’t been missed or mis-translated, so we would love to get feedback from Russian speakers on the quality of the work performed.</p>  <p>The below information has been provided by our LS team for people who are interested in taking Russian Homestyler for a spin (and ideally providing feedback, of course).</p>  <p>Be warned that these steps involve editing a core system file, and so clearly require a certain level of comfort with making such changes as well as appropriate permissions on your testing machine.</p>  <ol>   <li>Open the following file with a text editor (requires administrator privileges): </li> </ol>  <blockquote>   <p>%windir%\System32\drivers\etc\hosts</p> </blockquote>  <ol start="2">   <li>Add the following entries to the file: </li> </ol>  <p><font face="Courier New">107.22.29.153 www.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com      <br />107.22.29.153 ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 it.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 ru.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 en.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 es.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 pt.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br />107.22.29.153 fr.ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com       <br /></font></p>  <ol start="3">   <li>Open <a href="http://ec2-107-22-29-153.compute-1.amazonaws.com" target="_blank">this page</a> in your browser. </li>    <li>Choose Russian from the language drop-down in the top right corner: </li> </ol>  <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e20162ffc5ab15970d-pi" target="_blank"><img style="background-image: none;
