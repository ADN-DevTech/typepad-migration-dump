<p>I’ll be in Prague – with Adam Nagy from the ADN team – on Tuesday of next week (from 6 to 9pm on September 8, 2015) to talk to people about Autodesk’s horizontal PaaS offerings, notably the AutoCAD I/O and View &amp; Data web services. If you happen to be in the area and are interested in attending, please <a href="http://www.meetup.com/I-Love-DWG-Prague/events/225070857/" target="_blank">sign up</a> and come on by the Autodesk Prague office on Tuesday evening!</p>  <p>From my side I’m going to talk about the implementation details relating to the use of Autodesk web services in <a href="http://jigsawify.com" target="_blank">Jigsawify</a> and <a href="http://vrok.it" target="_blank">Vrok-It</a>.</p>  <p>It should be a fun event – and those who would like to stay and chat will be most welcome to join us for dinner, afterwards.</p>  <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201bb086cba58970d-pi" target="_blank"><img title="Clouds over Prague" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 50px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" border="0" alt="Clouds over Prague" src="/assets/image_28709.jpg" width="347" height="264" /></a></p>  <p>For more details check out <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/2015/09/i-love-dwg-meetup-in-prague.html" target="_blank">Adam’s post</a> on the AutoCAD DevBlog and <a href="http://thebuildingcoder.typepad.com/blog/2015/09/realdwg-autocad-io-view-data-api-meetup.html" target="_blank">Jeremy’s post</a> on The Building Coder. Hoping to see some of you next week in Prague!</p>
