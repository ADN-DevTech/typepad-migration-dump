<p>After the first two successful <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2014/11/2015-autodesk-cloud-accelerator.html" target="_blank">Cloud</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2015/04/autodesk-cloud-accelerator-v2-updated-developer-portal.html" target="_blank">Accelerators</a> held in San Francisco, it’s time to go international. The next one, labeled a <a href="http://autodeskcloudaccelerator.com/prague/" target="_blank">Cloud Accelerator Extension</a>, is being held in the beautiful Czech city of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/?title=Prague" target="_blank">Prague</a> from September 13-18, 2015.</p>  <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b7c7a399a1970b-pi" target="_blank"><img title="Clouds over Prague" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 20px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" border="0" alt="Clouds over Prague" src="/assets/image_672309.jpg" width="404" height="307" /></a></p>  <p>This is a great opportunity to kick-start your development using Autodesk’s <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Platform_as_a_service" target="_blank">PaaS</a> infrastructure. Members of the <a href="http://autodesk.com/adn" target="_blank">ADN</a> and Engineering teams – me included – will be on-hand to help you work through any development issues you hit while creating or honing your Autodesk Cloud API-integrated application.</p>  <p>So come join me in Prague for a fun week of cloud-oriented development. All you need to do is <a href="http://autodeskcloudaccelerator.com/apply/" target="_blank">submit your application proposal</a> by <strong>July 14</strong>: if you are selected to be one of the 10 participating companies, Autodesk will cover accommodation costs for up to 2 attendees.</p>  <p><a href="http://autodeskcloudaccelerator.com/prague/" target="_blank">Find out more here</a>.</p>  <p><font color="#a5a5a5">photo credit: </font><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/33834913@N00/2631633298"><font color="#a5a5a5">Prague - Pont-Charles - 21-06-2008 - 6h52</font></a><font color="#a5a5a5"> via </font><a href="http://photopin.com"><font color="#a5a5a5">photopin</font></a><font color="#a5a5a5"> </font><a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nd/2.0/"><font color="#a5a5a5">(license)</font></a></p>
