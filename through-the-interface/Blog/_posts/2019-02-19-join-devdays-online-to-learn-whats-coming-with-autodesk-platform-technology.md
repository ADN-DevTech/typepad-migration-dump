<p>There’s <a href="https://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/2019/01/join-us-for-our-devdays-online-webinars.html" target="_blank">a series of webinars starting next week</a> that will help you get a grip on what’s coming with various Autodesk platforms, both cloud-based and desktop. It’s also a chance to catch up on some of the content that was presented at last year’s DevCons.</p><p><a href="https://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2022ad3e39585200b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="500" height="333" title="Fill the gap in your platform knowledge" style="margin: 30px auto; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Fill the gap in your platform knowledge" src="/assets/image_410215.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p>All the below webinars start at the same time: <strong>8am PST&nbsp; | 11am EST | 4pm GMT | 5pm CET</strong>. Don’t worry if that time’s inconvenient, recordings will be made available soon after the webinars are finished.
