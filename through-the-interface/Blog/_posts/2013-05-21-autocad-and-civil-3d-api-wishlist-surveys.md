<p>The ADN team has <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/2013/05/autocad-api-wishlist-survey-2013.html" target="_blank">recently</a> <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/infrastructure/2013/05/autocad-civil-3d-api-wishlist-survey-2013.html" target="_blank">announced</a> their annual API wishlist surveys for both <a href="https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/JWHQGTJ" target="_blank">AutoCAD</a> and <a href="https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/Civil3DAPIWishListSurveyY2013" target="_blank">AutoCAD Civil 3D</a>.</p>  <p>Our Engineering teams take the results of these surveys very seriously, and greatly appreciate the time you take to provide us with feedback on both our APIs and their related resources.</p>  <p>The Civil 3D survey closes on <strong>June 15th</strong>, while the one for AutoCAD closes on <strong>June 21st</strong>. Thanks in advance for your feedback!</p>
