<p>A quick note to mention another webinar that’s happening next Thursday, this one on <strong>Autodesk Exchange Apps</strong>.</p>  <p>AutoCAD’s (and a number of other Autodesk products’, of course) very own App Store is going from strength to strength, by all accounts: it’s had nearly 2 million visitors and upwards of 600,000 downloads, many of which have been paid for. There’s gold in them thar hills, AutoCAD developers! :-)</p>  <p>Anyway, for more specific details on this webinar – which is being hosted by Jim Quanci and Stephen Preston from the ADN team – you can check <a href="http://thebuildingcoder.typepad.com/blog/2014/05/aec-hackathon-in-new-york-and-new-developer-guide-url.html" target="_blank">Jeremy’s</a> or <a href="http://getcoreinterface.typepad.com/blog/2014/05/autodesk-exchange-apps-webinar-may-22nd-learn-about-exciting-new-features-that-bring-new-opportuniti.html" target="_blank">Kevin’s</a> blogs. Otherwise here are the essentials.</p>  <p><b>Webinar details:</b></p>  <ul>   <li>Date: Thursday, May 22<sup>nd</sup>, 2014 </li>    <li>Time: 8 a.m. PDT – 11 a.m. EDT – 4 p.m. BST – 5 p.m. CET </li>    <li>Duration: Approximately one hour </li>    <li>Open to anyone interested in publishing apps on Autodesk Exchange Apps</li> </ul>  <p><b>Registration:</b></p>  <p>Reserve your place by May 20th by sending an email to <a href="mailto:adn-training-worldwide@autodesk.com?subject=Exchange%20Apps%20webinar">adn-training-worldwide@autodesk.com</a> with 'Exchange Apps webinar' as the subject. Make sure you include:</p>  <ul>   <li>Your full name </li>    <li>Email address </li>    <li>Company name</li> </ul>  <p>If you are interested in the webinar but time zones or work commitments prevent you from attending, we invite you to register anyway, so that we can send you the link to the recording after the event.</p>  <p>Note: If you want a head start in learning how to publish apps, visit <a href="http://www.autodesk.com/developapps">www.autodesk.com/developapps</a>. If you have any questions, send an email to <a href="mailto:appsinfo@autodesk.com">appsinfo@autodesk.com</a>.</p>
