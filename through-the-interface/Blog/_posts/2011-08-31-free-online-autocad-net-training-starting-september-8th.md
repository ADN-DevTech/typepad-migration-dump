<p>Starting next week – and for 8 weeks – Wayne Brill, from DevTech Americas, will be delivering free online AutoCAD .NET training.</p>  <p>To register – yes, <strong>for free – </strong>go to <a href="http://autodesk.com/apitraining" target="_blank">our API Training page</a> and select “Schedule”, then scroll down to “AutoCAD .NET”, “English”, “Webcast”, “Sep 08 2011” and click “Register” at the far right of the item.</p>  <p>This training is a live-delivered version of <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/05/new-self-paced-autocad-net-training-material.html">Wayne’s</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/05/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-2-user-interaction-user-input.html">excellent</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/05/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-3-database-fundamentals.html">series</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/05/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-4-database-events-paletteset.html">of</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/06/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-5-dictionaries-containers-type-identification-casting-1.html">DevTV</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/06/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-5-dictionaries-containers-type-identification-casting.html">sessions</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/06/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-7-jigs.html">posted</a> <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/06/autocad-net-training-devtv-session-8-user-interface-elements.html" target="_blank">previously</a> (<a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2011/07/transcripts-for-the-autocad-net-training-devtvs.html" target="_blank">with transcripts</a>). This is likely to appeal to those of you who like knowing there’s a warm body at the other end of their webcast link during a training session. And one that is very happy to interact with the audience and answer any questions you might have.</p>  <p>Enjoy! :-)</p>
