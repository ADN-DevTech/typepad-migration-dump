<p>In <a href="https://youtu.be/HzD4q1kINak" rel="noopener" target="_blank">this week’s episode</a> we take a look at four games, with a brief interlude of <a href="https://csdb.dk/release/?id=71275" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Nothin’ Special</a>. The first game was a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pac-Man" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Pac-Man</a> clone that’s apparently called <a href="https://www.mobygames.com/game/munch-man-64" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Munch Man 64</a>. We then went on to play two “patrol” games (which basically have nothing in common beyond the name): <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Falcon_Patrol_II" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Falcon Patrol II</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Park_Patrol" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Park Patrol</a>. We wrap up by playing <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ace_of_Aces_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Ace of Aces</a>, albeit - once again - very poorly.</p>
