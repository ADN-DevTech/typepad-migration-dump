<p>Yesterday afternoon I popped down to the <a href="http://www.case-a-chocs.ch/" target="_blank">Case à Chocs</a> in Neuchâtel to see the first of the robots being set up for tomorrow’s big event – <a href="http://autodesk.com/designnightswitzerland" target="_blank">Design Night Switzerland</a>, celebrating 25 years of Autodesk’s presence here.
