<p>We’ve been working on translating several of our <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/devtv/">DevTV</a> sessions, and our first Portuguese DevTV is now ready for consumption. :-)</p>  <p>A member of our DevTech Americas team based in São Paulo, Augusto Gonçalves, created this session based on Fenton Webb’s original&#160; (which you can get to from <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2007/11/devtv-introduct.html">this previous post</a>). Augusto translated the slides into Brazilian Portuguese, and provided his own voice-over for these and for Fenton’s demonstrations. Augusto tells me that the content should also be understandable for Portuguese speakers from elsewhere (Portugal, for instance :-).</p>  <p>DevTV: Introdução à Programação AutoCAD.NET&#160;&#160;&#160; <a href="http://download.autodesk.com/media/adn/DevTV_Introducao_a_Programacao_AutoCAD_NET/">view</a>&#160;&#160; <a href="http://download.autodesk.com/media/adn/DevTV_Introducao_a_Programacao_AutoCAD_NET.zip">download (172 Mb)</a></p>  <p>A big thank you to Fernando Malard (of <a href="http://arxdummies.blogspot.com/">ObjectARX &amp; Dummies</a> fame) for reviewing the content before publishing.</p>  <p>We’re also planning to deliver Japanese and Russian versions of this DevTV. More on those, in due course. Oh, and if you’re interested in a Chinese version of this material, you can <a href="http://download.autodesk.com/media/adn/AutoCAD.NETwebcast.zip">download and watch this webcast recording (86.8 Mb)</a>.</p>
