<p>In <a href="https://youtu.be/-44UH131jPs" rel="noopener" target="_blank">this week’s episode</a> – the first of 2022 – we take a look at five Commodore 64 games, although we only get to play four of them.</p>
<p>We start with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bomb_Jack" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Bomb Jack</a>, then (<a href="https://www.keanw.com/2021/09/floppy-friday-47.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">once again</a>!) fail to load <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Test_Drive_(1987_video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Test Drive</a>, before playing <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ace_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Ace</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pitstop_II" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Pitstop II</a> and wrap things up with another Hewson Consultants classic, <a href="https://www.gamesdatabase.org/game/commodore-64/eliminator" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Eliminator</a>.</p>
