<p>In today’s episode of Floppy Friday I’m happy to say that we’re back on floppies after a couple of weeks focused on cartridge games and unboxing. We play three games, this week: <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Predator_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Predator</a> (a trainer for it, anyway), <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_McGuigan_World_Championship_Boxing" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Barry McGuigan World Championship Boxing</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghosts_%27n_Goblins_(video_game)" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Ghosts ‘n Goblins</a>.</p>
