<p>In <a href="https://youtu.be/Q1qyCKlsyKE" rel="noopener" target="_blank">this week’s episode</a> we start with a few obscure German games (no offence to my German friends – this is a snap judgment based on a few web searches), called <a href="https://www.c64games.de/phpseiten/spieledetail.php?filnummer=178" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Katz und Maus</a> and then <a href="http://www.gamebase64.com/game.php?id=6500" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Rudi the Rat</a>, before finding a floppy with some more interesting fare: <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/River_Raid" rel="noopener" target="_blank">River Raid</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sub_Hunt" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Sub Hunt</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raid_over_Moscow" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Raid over Moscow</a>. As usual my ability to play these various games was highly variable, but hopefully you’ll get a sense of – and perhaps even some nostalgia from seeing – how they play.</p>
