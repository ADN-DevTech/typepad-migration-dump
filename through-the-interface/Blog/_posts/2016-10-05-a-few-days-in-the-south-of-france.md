<p>Our eldest son has caught the scuba diving bug, big-time. He’s a fourth generation scuba diver on my wife’s side – my side’s full of landlubbers – and as he’s just completed his PADI Junior Open Water it was important for us to find a good diving location for this October’s break. We chose a small town just south-west of Cannes, as the local diving centre – <a href="http://centre-plongee-rague.com/" target="_blank">le Centre de Plongée de la Rague</a> – is run by friends of friends originating from the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neuch%C3%A2tel" target="_blank">Neuchâtel</a> area.</p> <p>We’ve been here since Sunday and will leave on Friday – a short trip, but very welcome.</p> <p>On the way down we stopped for lunch in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avignon" target="_blank">Avignon</a> – somewhere it’s probably been 20 years since I visited. Luckily medieval towns don’t change very quickly, so I still knew my way around, to some degree.</p> <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b7c89bd88b970b-pi" target="_blank"><img title="Avignon steps" style="border-left-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom-width: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 30px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border-top-width: 0px" border="0" alt="Avignon steps" src="/assets/image_638390.jpg" width="281" height="500"></a></p> <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201b7c89bd894970b-pi" target="_blank"><img title="Sur le pont, d'avignon" style="border-left-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom-width: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 30px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border-top-width: 0px" border="0" alt="Sur le pont, d'avignon" src="/assets/image_239931.jpg" width="500" height="281"></a></p> <p>When we arrived at our Airbnb in <a href="http://www.mandelieu.fr/" target="_blank">Mandelieu-La-Napoule</a>, we found it to be a very nice place. I’m not much of a diver – my 12 year-old and I both did our 19th dives together on Monday (he has since surpassed me) – but it’s good for me to get back out there. We’ll be doing one last dive all together – including an open sea “baptism” for our 10 year-old – tomorrow, before we head back home.</p>
