<p>After the <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2013/07/autodesk-exchange-apps-portathon-code-for-cash.html" target="_blank">initial Autodesk Exchange Apps Portathon</a> was held last year, the ADN team is running a similar – but expanded – event, the <a href="http://exchangeapphack.com" target="_blank">Autodesk Exchange Apps Hackathon</a>, on September 20-21.</p>
<p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e201a3fd42be9f970b-pi" target="_blank"><img alt="Autodesk Exchange Apps Hackathon" border="0" height="209" src="/assets/image_678866.jpg" style="background-image: none; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 20px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Autodesk Exchange Apps Hackathon" width="464" /></a></p>
<p>There’s more information available on both <a href="http://thebuildingcoder.typepad.com/blog/2014/08/autodesk-exchange-apps-hackathon.html" target="_blank">Jeremy’s blog</a> and <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/autocad/2014/08/an-online-event-with-an-opportunity-to-receive-up-to-us-100-per-app-published-and-win-one-of-eight-i.html" target="_blank">the ADN DevBlog</a>, but here’s a quick summary of the salient points:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Free</strong> to attend</li>
<li>Open to <strong>anyone</strong>, <strong>anywhere</strong> (it’s a virtual event)</li>
<li>Lots of great <strong>presentations</strong> on app development</li>
<ul>
<li>The ADN team will also be available to provide <strong>support</strong></li>
</ul>
<li>You earn <strong>money</strong> for apps you get published</li>
<ul>
<li>$50/$100 for published free/paid apps, up to 5 per company</li>
</ul>
<li>Additional Cloud/Sustainability App <strong>competitions</strong>, with iPad minis to win</li>
</ul>
<p>This is really a great opportunity to get your apps in front of lots of Autodesk customers – there have been upwards of the <em>2.25 million</em> unique visitors to the store since its inception – and to earn money from Autodesk for doing so! Find out more from <a href="http://exchangeapphack.com" target="_blank">the Hackathon site</a>.</p>
