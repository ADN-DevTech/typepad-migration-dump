<p>Members of the <a href="http://autodesk.com/joinadn" target="_blank">Autodesk Developer Network</a> have been busily uploading videos to the new <a href="http://www.youtube.com/adnpartnerchannel" target="_blank">ADN Partner Channel</a> on YouTube.</p>  <p><a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e2013484e130c6970c-pi"><img style="border-right-width: 0px; margin: 20px auto 30px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px" title="The ADN Partner Channel on YouTube" border="0" alt="The ADN Partner Channel on YouTube" src="/assets/image_247244.jpg" width="455" height="332" /></a> </p>  <p>If you’re an Autodesk customer, interested in the breadth of products provided by our partners, then be sure to check it out.</p>  <p>If you’re an ADN member – and are interested in uploading your own videos to the channel – take a look at the <a href="http://adn.autodesk.com/adn/servlet/file/youtube_adnchannel_stater_guide.pdf?siteID=4814862&amp;id=15060436" target="_blank">YouTube ADN Channel Starter Guide</a> (login required) that explains the submission process in detail. [This document can be found on <a href="http://adn.autodesk.com" target="_blank">the ADN site</a> under the <a href="http://adn.autodesk.com/adn/servlet/autoindex?siteID=4814862&amp;id=4832177" target="_blank">Business &amp; Marketing</a> section.]</p>  <p>We’re working to include links to these videos on the <a href="http://autodesk.com/partnerproducts" target="_blank">Autodesk Partner Products &amp; Services</a> website, a commonly used source of information on the solutions and services provided by Autodesk’s partners.</p>
