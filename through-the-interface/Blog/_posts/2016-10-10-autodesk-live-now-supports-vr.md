Here’s a video showing what’s what:</p> <p align="center">&nbsp;</p> <p align="center"><iframe height="281" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/1hB2IMns1qw?rel=0&amp;showinfo=0" frameborder="0" width="500" allowfullscreen></iframe></p> <p align="center">&nbsp;</p> <p>This is great timing, as I’ve just heard that <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2016/09/vristmas-is-coming.html" target="_blank">my Demons</a> are at the doorstep (well, Zurich airport). I’ll for sure be kicking the tyres on this workflow over the coming weeks.</p> <p>If you’re curious in trying the beta for yourself: you should already have access to it, if you’re currently a <a href="http://www.autodesk.com/products/live/subscribe" target="_blank">LIVE subscriber</a>, otherwise you can <a href="http://www.autodesk.com/products/live/free-trial" target="_blank">try it out for 30 days</a>.
