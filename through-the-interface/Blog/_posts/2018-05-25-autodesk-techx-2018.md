<p>This week I’ve been in Detroit for Autodesk’s annual internal share-fest, TechX. In previous year’s we’ve had Technical Summits and X-Summits (for eXperience), but this year is the first combined event welcoming 1,000 Autodesk employees to the Motor City.</p><p>A lot of the content from the event is – of course – internal, but here are some shots I can share.</p><p>Being a Delta hub, flights to Detroit from my part of the world mostly seem to go via Amsterdam or Paris.
