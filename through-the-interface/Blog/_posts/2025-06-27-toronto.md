<p>This week I've been in Toronto for an internal summit of our Research Engineering team. On Monday I stopped at the SWISS lounge by the E gates at Zurich airport. It was nice to hang out on the balcony overlooking the planes... they even have binoculars you can borrow!<a target="_blank" href="/assets/image_904188.jpg" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><img alt="Outside area overlooking the planes." border="0" height="375" src="/assets/image_904188.jpg" style="display: block; margin: 30px auto;" title="Outside area overlooking the planes" width="500" /></a></p>
