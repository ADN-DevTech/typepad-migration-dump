<p><a href="https://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e202c1b257e3e9200d-pi" target="_blank"><font size="2"><img width="500" height="281" title="Munich" style="margin: 30px auto 10px; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Munich" src="/assets/image_656327.jpg" border="0"></font></a></p><p align="center"><font color="#a5a5a5" size="2">Photo © Michael Šubák from Pixabay</font></p><p>Hopefully those of you interested in attending the Autodesk DevCon 2023 in Munich, Bavaria, were able to <a href="https://www.keanw.com/2023/06/save-the-date-autodesk-devcon-in-munich-on-september-11-12.html" target="_blank">save the date</a> of September 11th-12th.</p><p>Well, it’s now possible to <a href="https://aps.autodesk.com/blog/register-autodesk-devcon-2023" target="_blank">register for the event</a>. There is an attendance fee – with early-bird pricing of 180€ up until July 30th, with the price going up to 300€ and then 360€ at the event itself – but hopefully there are fewer capacity limits on attendance than <a href="https://www.keanw.com/2023/05/register-today-for-autodesk-devcon-2023-in-sf.html" target="_blank">in SF</a> (one of the advantages of not being on a boat ;-).</p><p>It’s a fun time to be in Munich, with <a href="https://www.oktoberfest.de/en/information/when-is-oktoberfest" target="_blank">Oktoberfest starting the following weekend</a> on September 16th.</p><p><a href="https://through-the-interface.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83452464869e202c1a6cc0179200b-pi" target="_blank"><img width="501" height="333" title="Oktoberfest" style="margin: 30px auto 10px; border: 0px currentcolor; border-image: none; float: none; display: block; background-image: none;" alt="Oktoberfest" src="/assets/image_17687.jpg" border="0"></a></p><p align="center"><font color="#a5a5a5" size="2">Photo © Heribert Pohl, </font><a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0" target="_blank"><font color="#a5a5a5" size="2">CC BY-SA 2.0</font></a></p><p align="left">So if you enjoy large beers and crowds of lederhosen-wearing revellers, think about making a trip of it!</p>
