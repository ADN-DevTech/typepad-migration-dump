<p>I've <a href="http://through-the-interface.typepad.com/through_the_interface/2008/03/a-simple-taxono.html" target="_blank">mentioned before</a> how much I appreciate the content on <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/" target="_blank">MSDN's Channel 9</a>.</p>

<p>For those of you who are familiar with C# 1.0, but haven't yet taken the time to dive into the language features introduced since then, I strongly recommend the &quot;whirlwind&quot; series posted on Channel 9.</p>

<p>Here's a quick description of what these whirlwinds are all about:</p><blockquote><p><em>Whirlwinds are bite-sized webcasts, each is shorter than 15 minutes. You can start anywhere in the series to learn about the parts you're most interested in.</em></p></blockquote><p>Here are links to the individual whirlwinds:</p>

<ul><li><strong>What's new in C# 2</strong><ul><li><a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/Showpost.aspx?postid=390556" target="_blank">Whirlwind 1 - Generics</a></li>

<li><a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/ShowPost.aspx?PostID=391977" target="_blank">Whirlwind 3 - Partial Types, Anonymous Methods</a></li>
