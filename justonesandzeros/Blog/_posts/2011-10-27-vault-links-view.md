<p>Jan Liska has provided us with another Vault sample.&#0160; This one is a custom tab that shows all the links to the selected entity.&#0160; It works with files, folders, items or change orders.</p>
<p><img alt="" src="/assets/screenshot.png" /></p>
<p>The Where Used tab for files also has the ability to show links.&#0160; However it only applies to files, and it doesn&#39;t show the full folder path.&#0160; The new Links tab displays the full folder structure.</p>
<hr noshade="noshade" style="color: #013181;" />
<p><strong>Requirements:</strong> <br />Vault Workgroup/Collaboration/Professional 2012</p>
