<p>I updated Advanced Advanced Find to the 2014 release and made some improvements along the way.&#0160; I briefly toyed with the idea of calling it Advanced Advanced <em>Advanced</em> Find, but that would just be silly. </p>
<p>If your not familiar with the app, it works much like the advanced find feature in Vault Explorer.&#0160; But my app adds the ability to do OR searches across multiple properties.</p>
<p><a href="http://justonesandzeros.typepad.com/images/2013/AdvancedAdvancedFind/Dialog-full.png" target="_blank"><img alt="" src="/assets/Dialog-scaled.png" /></a>    <br /><span style="color: #666666;">(click for full size image)</span></p>
<p>New for 2014:</p>
<ul>
<li>Save/load feature.&#0160; </li>
<li>Results are shown in a <a href="http://justonesandzeros.typepad.com/blog/2013/06/the-grid-control.html" target="_blank">Vault Grid Control</a>. </li>
<li>Double-clicking a result closes the dialog and takes you to the object. </li>
</ul>
