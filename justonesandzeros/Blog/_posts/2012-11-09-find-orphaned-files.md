<p>While I’m busy getting my AU classes in order, Jan Liska kindly provided this month’s sample app.&#0160; He is also the author of the popular <a href="http://apps.exchange.autodesk.com/VLTC/Detail/Index?id=appstore.exchange.autodesk.com%3adrawingcompare%3aen" target="_blank">Drawing Compare</a> app.&#0160; Thanks Jan!</p>
<p>Find Orphaned Files is a quick and easy way to detect files that are not referenced by any other file.&#0160; In other words, files with no files listed in the “Where Used” tab.&#0160; To launch the app, just select a folder in Vault, right-click and choose the Find Orphaned Files command.</p>
