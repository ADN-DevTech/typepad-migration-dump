<p>I stayed in the

<a href="http://www.wiwanni.ch">Wiwanni hut</a> in the

<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valais">Canton of Valais</a> with

two friends and my son Cornelius, who returned from his studies of

<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_medicine">Traditional Chinese Medicine</a> in

<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>,

China, for a one-month visit in Europe.</p>

<p>We warmed up on the Salamander route to the summit of the Klein Augstkummenhorn (2880 m), then climbed the classical Steinadler route on the south face of the

<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiwannihorn">Wiwannihorn</a> (3001 m),

scrambling down the normal route to return to the hut and back home.</p>

<h4>Autodesk View and Data API Presentation</h4>

<p>I am presenting the

<a href="http://thebuildingcoder.typepad.com/blog/2014/07/autodesk-view-and-data-api.html">
Autodesk View and Data API</a> at the

<a href="http://www.meetup.com/basel-js/events/192651262">Basel.js meetup</a> hosted by

<a href="http://www.magnolia-cms.com">Magnolia International</a> in

<a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=St+Johanns-Vorstadt+38%2C+Basel%2C+ch">St. Johanns-Vorstadt 38, Basel, Switzerland</a>,

tomorrow evening at 19:00 CET and Swiss time.</p>

<p>It would be really great to see you there!</p>
