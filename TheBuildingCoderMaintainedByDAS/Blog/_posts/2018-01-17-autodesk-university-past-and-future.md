<h4><a name="3"></a>CFP for AU London 2018</h4>

<p>Autodesk University is coming to London again, for the second time, once again being held at Tobacco Dock, on June 19th and 20th.</p>

<p>For an impression of last year's event, check out 
the <a href="http://au.autodesk.com/au-online/classes-on-demand/search?full-text=AU+London&amp;year=2017&amp;conference=London">online overview of 2017 sessions</a>.</p>

<p>The CFP or <a href="https://aulondon2018.dryfta.com">call for proposals for this year's AU London</a> is now open, and runs through to the end of January.</p>

<p>If you'd like to speak at this year's event, <a href="https://aulondon2018.dryfta.com/en/abstract-submission">propose your class now</a>.</p>
