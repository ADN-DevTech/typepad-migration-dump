<p>Renzo van Rijswijk just pointed out that he offers some free Revit add-ins on his blog

<a href="http://revittoday.blogspot.com">
RevitToday</a>:

<ul>
<li><a href="http://revittoday.blogspot.com/2010/10/import-coordinates-into-revit-2011.html">
Import coordinates into Revit 2011</a> for 

importing coordinates from a TXT file. 
You are free to select a family of your own. 
It should be a Point Based family of course.
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="http://revittoday.blogspot.com/2010/09/export-xyz-from-revit-2011.html">
Export XYZ from Revit 2011</a>  to 

export all coordinates of Point Based Generic Models to an Excel file.
You can locate Point Based Generic Models in your whole model. 
You are able to nest such a Family within another. 
These points are exported as well when you make this family Shared.

</ul>
