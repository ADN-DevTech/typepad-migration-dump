<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/manufacturing/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p>Two planar faces which are coplanar are not always overlapped. To know if they are overlapped and the touching area, one workaround could be extrude the sketches based on the faces with a tiny distance, and use TransientBRep.DoBoolean to check if the bodies of the two extrude features are intersected. </p>  <p>The following is demo code. It assumes two planar faces are selected. One material named “Magenta” is available.</p>  <p>Note: in UI, if the two planar faces are within one surface body and are overlapped, they cannot be selected independently. So to make a demo, it assumes the two planar faces are from different bodies.
