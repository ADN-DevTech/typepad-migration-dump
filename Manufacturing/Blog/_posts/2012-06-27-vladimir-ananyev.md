<p>Vladimir joined Autodesk in April 2012, is based out of our Moscow office, and is currently focusing on providing technical and evangelism customization support for Inventor in the Russia and CIS region.</p>
<p>As a former ADN partner, Vladimir has also been running an independent Inventor wiki-book in Russia, and has been a keen exponent of Autodesk technology for a long time.</p>
<p>Vladimir has an MSc in the ‘Physics of Metals’ from the Moscow Institute of Steel and Alloys, and a Ph.D. in ‘Physical Metallurgy and Heat Treatment of Aluminium Alloys’ from the All-Russia Institute of Light Alloys.</p>
<p>Vladimir lives in Moscow with his family and in his spare time enjoys swimming and badminton.</p>
