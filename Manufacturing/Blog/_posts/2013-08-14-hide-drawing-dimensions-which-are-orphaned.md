<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/manufacturing/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p>For some reasons such as the corresponding components are removed/suppressed, the dimensions which ever attach to the components will be orphaned and can be removed.</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b019104c2527d970c-pi"><img style="border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px" title="image" border="0" alt="image" src="/assets/image_747e62.jpg" width="312" height="294" /></a> </p>  <p>Recently, I got a question that the user wants to hide the orphaned dimension. Once the components are available again, he wants to display the dimension again.</p>  <p><strong>DrawingDimension.Attached</strong> indicates whether this dimension is attached to anything. But there is no property to control the visibility of the dimension. You can only remove it completely by <strong>DrawingDimension</strong>.<strong>Delete</strong></p>  <p>After some investigations,&#160; a workaround came to my brain.
