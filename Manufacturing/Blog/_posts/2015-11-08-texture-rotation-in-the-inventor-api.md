<p>Q: How can I access the rotation and scale data of the texture?</p>
<p>All these values are stored in the appearance asset in the Inventor document.&#0160; An asset is a collection of values of different types. They can be simple types like floats, integers, strings, and Booleans, but they can also be more complex types like color, filename, reference to other assets, textures, and choices. In this case we need to find texture and access its values.</p>
