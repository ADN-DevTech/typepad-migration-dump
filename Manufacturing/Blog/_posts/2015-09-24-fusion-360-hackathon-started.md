<p><a href="http://fusion360hackathon.com" target="_self">Fusion 360 Hackathon</a> has now officially started.</p>
<p>Yesterday we did the opening ceremony with Viru being master of ceremony, Patrick talking about <a href="http://www.autodesk.co.uk/products/fusion-360/overview" target="_self">Fusion 360</a> and the opportunities around it, Hazel and Anand talking about the <a href="https://apps.autodesk.com/en" target="_self">Autodesk Exchange App Store</a> and the benefits of using it, and last but not least you could hear&#0160;<a href="http://modthemachine.typepad.com/my_weblog/about-the-author.html" target="_self">Brian</a> and me. <br />Then we finished with a long Q/A session.</p>
<p>Just like all the sessions during the hackathon, this webcast was recorded as well and can be downloaded from <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/files/Why%20develop%20for%20Fusion-Sept-23.zip" target="_self">here</a> or watched on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dhfXwyl5ktI&amp;index=2&amp;list=PL4CYqBFEfhpKKsU8RO0EM-IyuaQQmNoAO" target="_self">youtube</a>.</p>
