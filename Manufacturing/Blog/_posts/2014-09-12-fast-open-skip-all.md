<p>By&#0160;<a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/manufacturing/adam-nagy.html" target="_self">Adam Nagy</a></p>
<p>When opening a drawing you have some options:</p>
<p><a class="asset-img-link" href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01b7c6de9d93970b-pi" style="display: inline;"><img alt="Fastopen" border="0" class="asset  asset-image at-xid-6a0167607c2431970b01b7c6de9d93970b img-responsive" src="/assets/image_698964.jpg" title="Fastopen" /></a></p>
<p>If you check the possible values of the <strong>Options</strong> argument of <strong>Documents.OpenWithOptions</strong> in the <strong>API Reference</strong> then you can find the one corresponding to<strong> &quot;Skip all unresolved files&quot;</strong> - it&#39;s named&#0160;<strong>&quot;SkipAllUnresolvedFiles&quot;</strong></p>
<p>You won&#39;t find the equivalent of <strong>&quot;Fast Open (Skip All)&quot;</strong>&#0160;listed though. It seems it was left out accidentally, but only from the <strong>API Reference</strong>. It is implemented in the <strong>API</strong>&#0160;and can be used. It&#39;s named<strong>&#0160;&quot;FastOpen&quot;</strong></p>
