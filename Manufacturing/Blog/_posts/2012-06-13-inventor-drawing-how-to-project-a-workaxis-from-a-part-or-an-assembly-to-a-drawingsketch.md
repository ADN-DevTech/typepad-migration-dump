<p><strong>Issue</strong></p>
<p>I would like to create a SketchLine inside a DrawingSketch that represents my WorkAxis inside the referenced assembly document.</p>
<p><a name="section2"></a></p>

<p>This VB.NET example is from a button on a form in a standalone exe that connects to Inventor from out of process.</p>
