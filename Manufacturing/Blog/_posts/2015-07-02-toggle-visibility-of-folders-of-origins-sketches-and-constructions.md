<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/manufacturing/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p>Although the items in the folders of Origins, Sketches and Constructions have the corresponding API objects, the visibility of the folders are controlled by the following flags:</p>  <p>rootComponent.isSketchFolderLightBulbOn   <br />rootComponent. isOriginFolderLightBulbOn    <br />rootComponent. isConstructionFolderLightBulbOn</p>  <p>The usage is quite straightforward. Just set True or False will be same to what end user clicks the folder bulb on / off.</p>  <p>&#160;</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01b7c7a82418970b-pi"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="image" border="0" alt="image" src="/assets/image_68bb4c.jpg" width="244" height="210" /></a></p>
