<p>By <a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/manufacturing/xiaodong-liang.html" target="_self">Xiaodong Liang</a></p>  <p>API provides the corresponding methods of UI to create chamfer feature. </p>  <p><em>ChamferFeatures.AddUsingDistance</em>: creates a new ChamferFeature defined by a specified distance. The required arguments are the distance and the edges.</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01b7c764759f970b-pi"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="clip_image001" border="0" alt="clip_image001" src="/assets/image_63ccf4.jpg" width="244" height="228" /></a></p>  <p><em>ChamferFeatures.AddUsingDistanceAndAngle</em>: creates a new ChamferFeature that is defined by a distance and at a specified angle to an input face. The required arguments are referred face, edges, distance and angle.</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01bb08087e19970d-pi"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="clip_image002" border="0" alt="clip_image002" src="/assets/image_b47910.jpg" width="172" height="244" /></a></p>  <p><em>ChamferFeatures.AddUsingTwoDistances</em>: creates a new ChamferFeature that is defined by two distances. The required arguments are referred face, edges, distance one and distance two.</p>  <p><a href="http://adndevblog.typepad.com/.a/6a0167607c2431970b01b7c76475b3970b-pi"><img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="clip_image003" border="0" alt="clip_image003" src="/assets/image_69cc7b.jpg" width="209" height="244" /></a></p>  <p>The code below demos the three methods. It assumes it is a part document.
